Details of cooperation

what do we offer

Our collaboration will start from modification the technical requirements, making suggestions from our side and adapting design files. This is necessary to clearer interactions between all system elements and to develop your application according the requirements

A dedicated team will be assigned to your project which will consist of Backend and Frontend Developers, iOS and Android Developers, QA Engineer and a Project Manager. All of them have remarkable experience in their area and possess in-depth knowledge in necessary technologies, frameworks and libraries. The team is completely customizable and you will pay for people you truly require. These developers will be focused on your project only.

We will provide you with any help regarding cloud storage, necessary APIs documentation and their integration, and deploying mobile applications to the App Store and Google Play.

Project coordination. Our experienced Project Manager will work closely with you during the development: he will keep you informed about all processes and report about the progress, provide you with schedule of milestones which you can manage upon mutual agreement, assist you on any other question. We use Trello for product coordination which allows you to monitor and control the development processes and receive reports as well. In that way, all the processes will be transparent for you.

Time and material/hourly basis

Project is delivered on a standard basis, payments conducted according to actually spent hours, more flexible than fixed budget. Particularly good for open-ended, long term projects where it is hard to make a solid estimation of required efforts or there is a need to constantly add new features.

fixed price

Project is delivered on a standard basis for fixed price. Used for projects with clear, detailed software requirements, specifications and agreed timeline for the project completion. Price is fixed according to the preliminary agreement.

Hourly basis model will be flexible and we can change requirements on the go (we don't need low-level specification and can start directly from the design creation). In this case, the development process will be shorter. With Fixed Price model the process is strict and we need to go through all stages without the ability to change requirements after design stage.
